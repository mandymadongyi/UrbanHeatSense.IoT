Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

How do I find my latitude and longitude?

You can use Google Maps. Tap and hold your deployment location to get the coordinates.

Privacy and Ethics

How is my privacy protected?

Your data is anonymized and used solely for research purposes. Photos are optional and will not be shared without your consent.

Has this project received ethical approval?

Yes, the project has been reviewed and approved by UCL's ethics board.

Data Availability

I don't see my data on the map after 3 days. What should I do?

If your data is not visible on the live map within 3 working days, please contact dongyi.ma.21@ucl.ac.uk.
